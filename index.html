HelmingSense — Intelligence at the Helm

Smart Sailing, Made Simple

HelmingSense is a modular, Raspberry Pi–based marine monitoring and awareness system, designed for sailors who want deeper insight into their vessel without breaking the bank or getting locked into closed, proprietary systems.

Created by a seasoned offshore cruiser, HelmingSense brings together the power of open-source platforms like Signal K and Node-RED with purpose-built marine-grade sensors. Whether you're at anchor, motor-sailing up-channel, or on passage offshore, HelmingSense delivers clear, reliable, real-time data — right where you need it.

What HelmingSense Does

  • Rudder angle and tiller position
  • Engine RPM and alternator output
  • Temperature at key points
  • Humidity, barometric pressure, and dew point
  • Boat attitude and motion (via optional IMU module)
